Academic Excellence 🎓

A rigorous and balanced curriculum designed to spark curiosity, deepen understanding, and inspire a lifelong love of learning.

Primary School
(Grades 1–5)

Building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and values through joyful, hands-on learning.

  • Core subjects: English, Math, Science
  • Arts, music, PE & world languages (Spanish/French)
  • Project-based & play-based exploration

Middle School
(Grades 6–8)

Encouraging independence, critical thinking, and passion discovery through deeper inquiry.

  • Advanced Math, Science, Language Arts
  • Electives: Coding, Robotics, Drama, Sports
  • Leadership, service learning & global trips

High School
(Grades 9–12)

Preparing students for top universities and global careers with advanced academics and real-world readiness.

  • IB Diploma & Advanced Placement courses
  • College counseling & SAT/ACT prep
  • Research labs, internships & innovation projects

Why Choose Greenwood Academics?

At GIS, academics extend beyond textbooks — fostering well-rounded students who excel in character, creativity, and competence.

🌟 Personalized Learning

Small class sizes ensure each student gets attention that matches their unique learning style and pace — blending mastery of fundamentals with creative exploration.

📚 Expert Faculty

Experienced educators from diverse backgrounds bring international best practices into our classrooms — passionate professionals who care deeply about student success.

🌍 Global Perspective

With students from over 40 nationalities and an international curriculum, Greenwood provides an immersive global learning environment preparing students for today’s interconnected world.

💻 Technology Integration

From robotics labs to digital literacy programs, we embrace innovative technology in teaching, preparing students to thrive in a dynamic, tech-driven world.

🤝 Community & Values

Emphasis on integrity, empathy, and service ensures our graduates are not only smart but also compassionate — citizens who uplift their communities.

🎓 Alumni Success

Guided by strong academics and leadership opportunities, our alumni now contribute to top universities and industries around the globe as changemakers.
